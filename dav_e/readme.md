# Dave's prime_e layout

This layout is a combination of the default prime_e mixed with a custom minivan.

## Default (0)

```
 ,-----------------------------------------.  ,---------------------------------------------------.
 | Gesc |   Q  |   W  |   E  |   R  |   T  |  |   Y  |   U  |   I  |   O  |   P  | Esc   | Bksp   |
 |-------|------|------|------|------|------|  |------|------|------|------|------|------+--------|
 | Tab    |   A  |   S  |   D  |   F  |   G  |  |  H   |   J  |   K  |   L  |   ;  | LT(2) Enter  |
 |---------|------|------|------|------|------|  |------|------|------|------|------|-------------|
 | ShiftCaps|   Z  |   X  |   C  |   V  |   B  |  | MO(3)|  N   |   M  |   ,  |  .   |  / |' Shift|
 |------|------|------|------|------|------|----|  |------------|------|------|------|------|-----|
 | Ctrl |  Alt |       |  GUI    | TG(1) Space  |  |   Space    |  GUI |       |TG(3)  |  MO(4)   |
 `----------------------------------------------'  `----------------------------------------------'
```

- Left shift has tap dance enabled; double tap for capslock
- Right shift is a single quote on tap, shift on hold
